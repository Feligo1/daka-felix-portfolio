Hello! I'm Daka Felix, known professionally as "Tech Guy". I'm a Zambian computer scientist with a passion for making technology accessible and relevant to the African context.

My journey in technology began in Zambia, where I developed a deep fascination with how computers work and how they can solve real-world problems. This curiosity evolved into a comprehensive expertise spanning multiple IT domains.

"I believe technology should be a tool for empowerment and development. My approach combines international technical standards with local understanding, ensuring solutions are not only effective but also sustainable and relevant to the Zambian and African context."

Continuous Learning

I'm committed to continuous professional development. Through platforms like Cisco Networking Academy and Huawei ICT Academy, I stay current with emerging technologies while building on my core expertise in networking, cybersecurity, and AI.

Why Work With Me?

I bring a unique combination of technical expertise and cultural understanding. As a Zambian tech professional, I understand both the challenges and opportunities in our digital landscape and can tailor solutions that truly work for our context.
